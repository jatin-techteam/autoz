Step-wise guide for Registration
  1. Create your username and password. Remember to keep it similar with your business name & easy to remember. ( Note it down somewhere else.)
  2. Complete your profile by providing the data as demanded on the screen. Profile completion is divided into 3 sections. All sections are mandatory to be filled.
    1. Section 1 (Personal Details)
      1. Enter your personal details. Be sure to fill the columns which are marked with asterisk (*).
      2. Upload the document required in PDF, JPEG,JPG, PNG format as mentioned in the field.
      3. Check your filled data again before submission. Then click “Save & Proceed”.
    2. Section 2 (Business Details) Enter your business details.
      1. Be sure to fill the columns which are marked with asterisk(*).
      2. Upload the document required in PDF, JPEG,JPG, PNG format as mentioned in the field.
      3. Check your filled data again before submission. Then click to accept our terms & conditions and Privacy Policy. The click on “Save & Proceed”.
    3. Section 3 (Verification)
      1. You will receive a reference number for the physical verification. Our representatives will reach to you within 24 hours to verify your business. Keep it handy, as it is to produced during verification done by our representatives.
      2. After the successful verification, login through your self generated username & password to view the verification status.
      3. If status shows successful verification, then click on “Start” button to view the dashboard.
